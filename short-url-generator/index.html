How to Use URL Shortener

Creating Short Links

  • Enter your long URL - Make sure to include http:// or https:// at the beginning
  • Custom alias (optional) - Create a memorable name for your link
  • Click "Create Short Link" - Your shortened URL will be generated instantly
  • Copy to clipboard - Use the copy button to easily share your link

Custom Alias Tips

  • Use only letters and numbers - Don't use dashes, underscores, spaces or other symbols
  • Keep it simple - Use single words like "mylink" instead of "my-link" or "my_link"
  • Be unique - If your alias is already taken, you'll need to choose another one
  • Be memorable - Choose something easy to remember and type

Managing Your Links

  • View your links - All your shortened links appear in the "Your Recent Links" section
  • Copy links - Click the copy icon to copy any of your previously created links
  • Delete links - Remove individual links with the delete button
  • Clear all - Remove all your links at once with the "Clear All" button

Note:

  • If you delete your link Or Clear all your Links it will be permantly deleted from database and you wouldn't be able to access again.
  • Your links are private and can only be seen on your device. If you use multiple devices, your links won't automatically sync between them.
  • We don't collect any personal information when you create short links.
  • Each device has its own unique ID that is stored locally on your device only.
  • Only you can see and manage the links you've created - no one else can access them.
  • Your browsing history and link usage are not tracked or monitored.
