Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2024

1. Information We Collect

When you use LinkShare, we collect the following information:

  • WhatsApp group links you submit
  • Group titles and descriptions
  • Categories you select for your groups
  • Timestamps of when groups are submitted

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Display WhatsApp groups on our platform
  • Categorize and organize groups
  • Improve our service
  • Prevent abuse and maintain security

3. Information Sharing

We do not sell or share your personal information with third parties. Group information submitted to our platform is publicly visible to all users.

4. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information:

  • Secure data storage using Firebase
  • Regular security audits
  • Limited access to internal systems

5. User Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your submitted group information
  • Request removal of your groups
  • Report inappropriate content

6. Cookies and Tracking

We use essential cookies to maintain basic site functionality. No third-party tracking cookies are used.

7. Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Significant changes will be notified through our website.

8. Contact Us

If you have questions about our privacy policy, please contact us through our contact page.
