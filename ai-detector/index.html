What is an AI Detector?

An AI detector is a powerful tool that analyzes text to determine whether it was written by artificial intelligence (like ChatGPT, GPT-4, Bard, or other AI models) or by a human. Our free AI detector uses advanced algorithms to identify patterns, writing styles, and linguistic markers commonly found in AI-generated content.

How Does AI Detection Work?

Our AI detector analyzes multiple factors including:

  • Writing Patterns: AI-generated text often follows predictable patterns and structures
  • Vocabulary Usage: AI tends to use certain words and phrases more frequently
  • Sentence Structure: Analysis of sentence complexity and variation
  • Coherence Analysis: How ideas flow and connect throughout the text
  • Stylistic Markers: Identifying unique characteristics of AI writing

What is AI Text Humanization?

AI text humanization is the process of transforming AI-generated content to make it sound more natural, conversational, and human-like. Our text humanizer tool rewrites AI content while maintaining the original meaning, making it:

  • More engaging and relatable
  • Less robotic and formulaic
  • Better suited for human readers
  • More likely to pass AI detection tools
